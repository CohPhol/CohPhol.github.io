How I Started...
The first step I took an everthing shower, if you dont know what an everything shower means it means i took every product I saw in the shower and I used it on me hahaha.... ok ok enough with the bad jokes, second step I clean my face with cosrx salicylic acid cleanser, while you wash your face make sure you scrub softly, third step I used the HMB green caviar cream moisturizer when using this you only need a small amount because this product is a water base moisturizer, fourth step add your prefered sunscreen! sunscreen is really important because it protects you from the harmful UV rays the sun gives you! so thats all for my skin care.
Now we go off to my makeup routine, I add my concealer the blk cosmetics daydream life-proof airy concealer to my under-eye, t zone. After adding my concealer I use a beauty sponge to dab it all over my face, then I put contour, I used the careline contour stick, once you put it in ur fingertips I outline my nose to give it definition and I put a line on both of my cheeks then i blend the contour all over my face, for my eyeshadow i used the Too Faced Chocolate Bon Bons Chocolate Bar Eye pallete, now i add my final step to my makeup routine I used the sunnies face airblush shade cherub aroung my cheeks. thats it for my Get Ready with Me Party Look thank you all so much!
